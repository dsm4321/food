Ingredients

  1. 3 packs Rolo chocolate candies
  2. 30 mini pretzels
  3. 30 nuts (pecans, almonds, hazelnuts or walnuts), toasted
    4.






Directions

  1. Preheat the oven to 350F.
  2. Upwrap the Rolo's. Place pretzels on a baking sheet. Top each with a Rolo.
  3. Bake in the oven for 3-5 minutes, until the chocolate just begins to melt. The Rolo should be soft but not completely melted..
  4. Remove from oven, place on cooling rack and immediately squish the chocolate with a nut. Watch the caramel ooze out.
  5. IMPORTANT: Eat as many as you can while they are still warm. Go ahead, I give you permission to stuff yourselves with sugar.
